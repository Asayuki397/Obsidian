- n! = the number of permutation $\{ 1, 2, \dots, n \}$
- $nCr = \begin{pmatrix}n\\r\end{pmatrix}$
- ex) In a box, there are 6 type A balls, 9 type B balls. calculate $P(3A, 2B)$
$$
\frac{\begin{pmatrix}
6 \\ 3
\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}
9 \\ 2
\end{pmatrix}}{\begin{pmatrix}
15 \\ 5
\end{pmatrix}}
$$

4 \\ 2

$$
