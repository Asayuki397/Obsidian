## Nested Quantifiers
- ex ) $L(x,y)$ : $x$ loves $y$ 
- Everybody loves somebody.
	- For every person $x$ there exists a person $y$ such that $x$ loves $y$.
	$$
	\forall x \exists y \space L(x,y)
	$$
- How about this
$$
\exists x \forall y \space L(x,y)
$$
- *Not the same*

$$
\forall x \forall y ((x>0)\cap (y>0)) \rightarrow\space (x + y) > 0
$$
